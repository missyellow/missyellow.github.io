Voronezh

With Anastasia Vikhornova and Koby Barhad Voronezh is an administrative district of Russia. We redesigned the entry markers to the region. The original markers were constructed around mid century and they are falling apart, there was no one strong symbol for region and the signs were often a confusing mess. We designed new signs based upon our symbols for the region, the symbols were designed to reference different qualities and aspects. The signs were designed to create a striking image and a strong sense of place..
