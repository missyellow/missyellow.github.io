Cult of the dodo

Set within a scenario where Increasing numbers species are being threatened with extinction. I designed a cult that seeks to reverse this trend ,using genetic engineering together with traditional religious practices it is working on bringing the dodo back from the dead.

The dodo is emblematic of species extinction. It happened with such rapidity that it never stood a chance. But it happened recently enough that enough information remains to make a resurrection attempt a plausible possibility.
