Springposium Symposium

An instillation with Anastasia Vikhornova for the Maslenitsa celebration in Gorky park in central Moscow.

Maslenitsa is a traditional Russian celebration marking the coming of spring and warmer times. We took inspiration from Stravinsky's ballet the right of spring, to make a playful interactive event.

Different actions performed on the set would trigger different sounds, for example pulling ropes or jumping on a mushroom. In this way the audience was encouraged to dance within and interact with the set creating there own musical composition. remains to make a resurrection attempt a plausible possibility.
